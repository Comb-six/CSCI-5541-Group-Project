# CSCI-5541-Group-Project
Feeling Machines: How Emotional Expression in GenAI Recommendations Shapes Consumer Decisionso

This study aims to explore how varying emotional expressions from GenAI chatbots influence consumer responses. Drawing on theories such as the Computer as Social Actors (CASA) framework, the Uncanny Valley hypothesis, and the Elaboration Likelihood Model (ELM), the study investigates how varying emotional intensity levels in AI recommendations affect engagement, satisfaction, and purchase behavior across different product categories.
